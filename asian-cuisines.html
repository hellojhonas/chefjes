Asian Cuisines

Below are some of the Asian Cuisines that Chef Jes can cook.

Filipino Cuisines

Filipino Cuisine is known for its bold flavors and wide range of influences from Chinese, Spanish, and even American. It features a variety of dishes from sweet desserts and traditional dishes like adobo and pancit. Interesting fact: Pork is the most popular meat in Filipino cuisine.

Japanese Cuisines

Japanese Cuisine is known for its emphasis on freshness and seasonality of ingredients. It has become popular all over the world for its elegant presentation and use of fish, rice, and vegetables. Interesting fact: The Japanese drink more green tea than any other nation in the world.

Chinese Cuisines

Chinese Cuisine is one of the oldest cuisines in the world and is known for its rich flavors and use of aromatic spices. It is characterized by its wide range of dishes and regional variations. Interesting fact: Chinese cuisine consists of over 8,000 different dishes.

Korean Cuisines

Korean Cuisine is composed of a wide variety of dishes and flavors, with a heavy emphasis on vegetables and fermented foods. It is known for its use of spicy flavors and its popular side dishes like kimchi. Interesting fact: The traditional Korean meal is called "Bansang", which includes 12 side dishes.

Malaysian Cuisines

Malaysian Cuisine is a unique blend of Chinese, Indian, and Malay flavors. It is known for its use of spices and herbs, as well as its wide range of delicious dishes from noodles to curries. Interesting fact: Malaysian cuisine is often cooked with a wok or steamer, making it a very healthy cuisine.

Thai Cuisines

Thailand Cuisine is a vibrant mix of flavors, offering delicious and unique dishes like Pad Thai and Tom Yum Soup. It uses a variety of herbs and spices to create complex and flavorful dishes. Interesting fact: Thai cuisine is known for its use of chilies and the balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy flavors.

Indian Cuisines

Indian Cuisine is characterized by its use of spices and herbs, as well as its delicious curries, tandoori dishes, and all manner of vegetarian dishes. Interesting fact: India is the world's largest producer of spices, and many of its dishes are flavored with a variety of spices.

Vietnamese Cuisines

Vietnamese Cuisine is known for its fresh, light flavors and a focus on seafood. It is characterized by its use of herbs and vegetables, as well as its popular noodle dishes like pho and bun. Interesting fact: Vietnamese cuisine is known for its balance of five fundamental flavors: spicy, sour, bitter, salty, and sweet.
