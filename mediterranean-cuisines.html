Mediterranean Cuisine

Mediterranean Cuisine is a mix of flavors from various countries, such as Italy, Greece, and Turkey. It is known for its use of fresh herbs, vegetables, and seafood, as well as its flavorful olive oil. Interesting fact: The Mediterranean diet has been linked to a number of health benefits, including reduced risk of heart disease and cancer.

Spanish Cuisine

Spanish Cuisine is known for its flavorful use of garlic, onions, and spices. It is characterized by its wide range of dishes, such as paella, tapas, and gazpacho. Interesting fact: Spanish cuisine is known for its use of fresh, seasonal ingredients, such as tomatoes, peppers, and olives.
