Below is a Full course Meal during the Degustation of Chef Jes

Cream of Broccoli Soup

Cream of Broccoli soup is a comforting, creamy soup made from boiled broccoli blended with a creamy broth. It's a great way to add some vegetables to a meal and can be topped with a variety of ingredients such as cheese, croutons, or bacon bits. This wholesome soup is perfect for a light lunch or dinner and can be enjoyed by itself or with a sandwich or salad.

Shrimp Potato Croquettes (Appetizer)

Shrimp Potato Croquettes are a delicious and easy-to-make recipe. They consist of mashed potatoes, shrimp, and cheese, all rolled into a crispy croquette and fried until golden brown. These croquettes are perfect as a side dish, appetizer, or snack.

Garden Salad with Mango and Vinaigrette Dressing

This garden salad is a light and refreshing mix of fresh greens, topped with juicy mango chunks and drizzled with a tangy vinaigrette dressing. The bright sweetness of the mango pairs nicely with the crunchy vegetables, creating a delicious, healthy meal that is sure to satisfy. Souse Sirloin Steak with Asparagus (Main)

Souse sirloin steak with asparagus is a delicious and healthy dish. It features juicy sirloin steak cooked to perfection, served with tender asparagus and a light gravy. The perfect balance of flavors makes this dish an ideal choice for a nutritious meal.

Assorted Cake Desserts

Assorted Cake for Desserts is a delicious collection of cakes that are perfect for any occasion. Each cake is made with the freshest ingredients and comes in a variety of flavors, including chocolate, vanilla, and red velvet. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or just want a tasty treat, Assorted Cake for Desserts has something for everyone.
