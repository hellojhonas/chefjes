Meet the Chef

Chef Jesreel Ambi

Nagwapo ken Nataraki

Chef Jes is a master of Japanese cuisine and bread and pastry. He has been cooking professionally for over 4 years and has perfected his craft. He has studied various Japanese cooking techniques and has a vast knowledge of Japanese ingredients. His bread and pastry creations are renowned for their unique flavor and texture. He sources only the freshest and highest quality ingredients to create masterpieces that wow the palate. He strives to continually push the boundaries of Japanese cuisine and is always looking for ways to make his dishes more interesting and flavorful. He is a passionate chef who loves to share his knowledge and experience with others.
